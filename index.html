SunSip Privacy Policy

Overview

SunSip is a tanning timer app that uses your location to provide UV index data and safe sun exposure recommendations. Your privacy is important to us.

Data We Collect

Location Data: We access your device's location solely to fetch the current UV index for your area. Your location is sent to the OpenUV API (openuv.io) to retrieve UV data, and to Apple WeatherKit for temperature and humidity. We do not store or share your location.

Apple Sign-In: If you choose to sign in with Apple, we store your name and email locally on your device using the system Keychain. This information is never transmitted to any server.

Preferences: Your skin type, SPF setting, and onboarding status are stored locally on your device using standard app storage. This data never leaves your device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect analytics, usage data, crash reports, advertising identifiers, or any health data. We do not have a server or database. All data stays on your device.

Third-Party Services

OpenUV API: Your approximate location (latitude/longitude) is sent to openuv.io to retrieve the current UV index. See their privacy policy at openuv.io/privacy.

Apple WeatherKit: Your approximate location is sent to Apple's weather service for temperature and humidity data. See Apple's privacy policy at apple.com/privacy.

Notifications

If you grant permission, we send local notifications to remind you to flip sides and when your session is complete. These are generated entirely on your device.

Children's Privacy

SunSip is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL and within the app.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at the email address listed on the App Store.
